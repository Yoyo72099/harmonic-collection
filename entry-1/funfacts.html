Full Name

Hello Kitty's full name is Kitty White.

Home

She lives in London with her parents and sister Mimi.

Birthday

Her birthday is November 1st, which makes her a Scorpio.

Favorite Food

She loves eating apple pie made by her mom.

School

She attends London's Royal Ballet School.

Hobbies

She enjoys baking, sewing, and collecting cute things.

Pet

She has a pet cat named Charmmy Kitty.

Height

She is five apples tall and three apples wide.
