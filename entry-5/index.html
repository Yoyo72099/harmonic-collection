Meet The Friends

My Melody

Hello Kitty's best friend who wears a red hood. She's kind and gentle, always willing to help others.

Fun fact: She loves baking cookies and collecting ribbons.

Cinnamoroll

A small white puppy with long ears that enable him to fly. He's shy but very friendly.

Fun fact: He was born on a cloud and can fly using his big ears.

Pompompurin

A golden retriever dog who loves to sleep and collect shoes. He's laid-back and friendly.

Fun fact: His favorite food is pudding, which inspired his name.

Kuromi

My Melody's playful rival with a mischievous personality. She loves writing in her diary.

Fun fact: She has a gang of friends called the Kuromi Gang.

Keroppi

A cheerful frog who lives in Donut Pond. He's active and loves playing sports with friends.

Fun fact: He has a brother named Keroppe and a sister named Keroleen.

Badtz-Maru

A penguin with a bad attitude but a good heart. He loves practical jokes and skateboarding.

Fun fact: He lives in Gorgeoustown with his pet alligator, Pochi.
